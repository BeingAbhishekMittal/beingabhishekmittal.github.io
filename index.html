About

A data architect who turns strategy into working platforms.

I help teams build data platforms that actually work for the business, not just tick off a bunch of technical requirements.

I have over twelve years designing and implementing data engineering and analytics solutions across Microsoft Azure, Snowflake, Databricks, Microsoft Fabric, and Power BI — for clients spanning the Middle East, Europe, South-East Asia, Oceania and the US.

I work end-to-end: running discovery and assessment workshops to define data strategy roadmaps, then architecting and delivering the platforms that bring them to life — data lakehouses, cloud warehouses, master data management, and self-service analytics that teams actually trust.

I take on a focused set of consulting engagements — data strategy, architecture, Snowflake and Azure builds, migrations, and MDM — for teams that want senior architectural judgment without a senior headcount.
